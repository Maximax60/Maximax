IMMIGRATION FRAUD ALERT – TPS & WORK PERMIT

MAXIMAX ap bay avètisman pou kominote a sou fo “èd imigrasyon” pou TPS, Asylum ak Work Permit. Tanpri li mesaj sa a an ANGLE ak KREYÒL pou pwoteje tèt ou ak fanmi w.

Fraud Warning (English)

Recently, more people report scammers using WhatsApp, Facebook, and text messages to sell fake immigration help for TPS, Work Permits, and Asylum.

  • No one can “guarantee” TPS, Work Permit, or Asylum approval.
  • USCIS does not ask you to pay fees by Zelle, CashApp, or gift cards.
  • Scammers may promise fast approval if you send money to a personal account.
  • Never sign a blank immigration form or a form you do not understand.
  • Always check official updates directly on uscis.gov or the Federal Register website.
  • Keep your receipt numbers, A-number, and personal documents private.

Alèt Fwòd Imigrasyon (Kreyòl)

Dènye tan sa yo gen plis moun k ap plenyen sou fo “ajans” k ap vann sèvis sou WhatsApp, Facebook, ak mesaj pou TPS, Work Permit, ak Asil.

  • Pèsonn pa ka garantri TPS, Work Permit oswa Asil pou ou.
  • USCIS pa janm mande pou w peye frè pa Zelle, CashApp, gift card elatriye.
  • Gen moun k ap pwomèt apwobasyon rapid si w voye lajan sou kont pèsonèl yo.
  • Pa janm siyen yon fòm vid oswa yon fòm ou pa konprann ditou.
  • Toujou verifye enfòmasyon dirèkteman sou uscis.gov oswa sit Federal Register la.
  • Kenbe nimewo resi ou, nimewo A ou, ak dokiman pèsonèl ou yo an sekirite.

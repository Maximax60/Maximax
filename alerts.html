MAXIMAX IMMIGRATION ALERT
TPS, Asylum & Work Permit – FRAUD WARNING

Fraud Warning (English)

MAXIMAX is warning the Haitian community about fake immigration “services” on WhatsApp, Facebook, and text messages.

Red flags to watch for:

  • People who promise “same day” TPS, Work Permit, or Asylum approval.
  • Anyone who tells you to send money by Zelle, Cash App, gift card, or Bitcoin.
  • People who fill out forms using false addresses, fake sponsors, or fake employers.
  • Anyone who refuses to give you a copy of the forms they submit in your name.

How MAXIMAX helps

  • We review USCIS letters and notices with you.
  • We help you file or renew TPS & Work Permit properly.
  • We help you understand deadlines so you don’t lose your case.

Alèt Fwòd Imigrasyon (Kreyòl)

MAXIMAX ap avèti kominote Ayisyen an sou fo “ajans imigrasyon” ki voye mesaj sou WhatsApp, Facebook, ak SMS.

Si ou wè sa yo, pran prekosyon:

  • Moun k ap pwomèt ou TPS, Work Permit, oswa Azil “menm jou a”.
  • Moun k ap mande w peye sèlman pa Zelle, Cash App, kat kado oubyen Bitcoin.
  • Moun k ap ranpli fòm ak fo adrès, fo sponsor, oswa fo patron.
  • Moun ki pa vle ba w kopi fòm yo ranpli sou non w.

Kijan MAXIMAX ka ede w

  • Nou revize lèt USCIS, TPS, Asil, ak Work Permit ansanm avè w.
  • Nou ede w ranpli oswa renouvle Work Permit san pran risk ak fo enfòmasyon.
  • Nou esplike dat limit yo pou ou pa pèdi dosye w san w pa konnen.

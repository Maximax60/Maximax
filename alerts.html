IMMIGRATION ALERT

Federal Register Update (English)

USCIS has released a major immigration fee update under H.R. 1 for FY 2025 & FY 2026.

  • New first-time EAD fee (TPS/Asylum): $550
  • Automatic EAD extensions will end after October 30, 2025

What this means:
If you are on TPS, renewing a Work Permit, or in an asylum process, your filings must be updated quickly to avoid delay or rejection.

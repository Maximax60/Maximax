Federal Register Update (English)

  • Topic: Work Permit (EAD) fee update for TPS / Asylum
  • New fee: $550 for first–time EAD applications
  • Automatic extensions end: after October 30, 2025

What this means:
If you are on TPS, renewing a Work Permit, or in an Asylum process, your filings must be updated to avoid delay or rejection.

Mizajou Federal Register (Kreyòl)

  • Sijè: Mizajou sou frè Work Permit (EAD) pou TPS / Azil
  • Nouvo frè: $550 pou premye demann Work Permit
  • Ekstansyon otomatik fini: apre 30 Oktòb 2025

Sak sa vle di:
Tout moun ki sou TPS, k ap renouvle Work Permit, oswa ki nan pwosesis Azil dwe mete dosye yo ajou rapid pou evite rejè oswa gwo reta.

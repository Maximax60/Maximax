Support with Immigration Forms & Documents

MAXIMAX Multiservices helps you understand and prepare your immigration paperwork with patience and clarity. We serve the Haitian and broader community in English, Kreyòl, and Español.

  • Family-based petition forms
  • Adjustment of Status packet preparation
  • Work permit (EAD) form assistance
  • Green card renewal forms
  • Citizenship (naturalization) form preparation
  • Support organizing evidence and documents

MAXIMAX Multiservices is a document preparation and support service. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

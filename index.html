Tax · IRS Refund Processing Time

Kreyòl: IRS di ranbousman ka pran anviwon 3–5 semèn pou moun ki depoze bonè, sitou si w reklame EITC oswa CTC. Sa pa vle di gen pwoblèm nan dosye a otomatikman.

English: IRS reports refunds may take about 3–5 weeks for early filers, especially those claiming EITC/CTC. That does not automatically mean something is wrong.

Who is affected: Early filers · Parents with dependents · Low-income workers

Who is affected: Adults without dependents · Low-income adults

ALERTE · Nouvo Lwa & Mizajou

Paj sa a bay enfòmasyon sou chanjman nan lwa imigrasyon, taks, SNAP, Section 8, Social Security, chomaj ak lòt pwogram. Se rezime senp pou ede w konprann sa k ap soti nan gouvènman an.

Immigration · New USCIS Fee Rule

Kreyòl: USCIS mete nouvo frè sou plizyè fòm (I-130, I-485, I-765, I-821, elatriye). Gen kèk frè ki monte, kèk ki bese. MAXIMAX ka ede w chèche frè ki kòrèk pou ka pa w la anvan ou voye dosye a.

English: USCIS updated fees for many forms (I-130, I-485, I-765, I-821, etc.). Some increased and some decreased. MAXIMAX can help you calculate the correct filing fee.

Who is affected: Family petitions • Work permits • Green card applicants • TPS cases

Tax · IRS Refund Processing Time

Kreyòl: IRS di ranbousman ka pran anviwon 3–5 semèn pou moun ki depoze bonè, sitou si yo reklame EITC oswa CTC. Sa pa vle di gen pwoblèm nan dosye a otomatikman.

English: The IRS reports refunds may take around 3–5 weeks for early filers, especially if claiming EITC/CTC. This does not automatically mean there is a problem.

Who is affected: Early filers • Parents with dependents • Low-income workers

Benefits · SNAP Work Requirement

Kreyòl: Gen nouvo kondisyon travay pou kèk adilt san timoun rete elijib pou SNAP. Gen eksepsyon pou moun ki gen andikap oswa ki rete nan zòn espesifik.

English: New work rules apply to some adults without dependents to keep SNAP eligibility, with exemptions for disabled individuals and certain areas.

Who is affected: Adults without dependents • Low-income adults

Housing · Section 8 Waitlist

Kreyòl: Kèk ajans lojman lokal ap relouvri lis atant Section 8 yo. Dat ak kondisyon yo depann de vil ou. Li enpòtan pou w aplike vit lè lis la ouvè.

English: Some local housing authorities are reopening Section 8 waitlists. Dates and requirements vary by city. Apply quickly once the list opens.

Who is affected: Low-income families • Seniors • Disabled individuals

Social Security · COLA Increase

Kreyòl: Benefisyè SSI ak Reta ap resevwa yon ogmantasyon nan benefis yo (COLA) pou ajiste ak pri lavi a. Sa vle di chèk ou ap ogmante yon ti kras chak mwa.

English: SSI and Social Security beneficiaries will receive a cost-of-living adjustment (COLA), so monthly payments increase slightly.

Who is affected: SSI recipients • Social Security retirees • Disabled beneficiaries

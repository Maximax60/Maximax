Immigration · Filing Fees Update

Immigration · TPS & Work Permit Window

Kreyòl: Gen peryòd limite pou renouvle TPS ak travay ou. Si ou rate dat limit la, sa ka mete dosye w an reta oswa kreye risk pou estati w.

English: TPS and work permit renewals have strict deadlines. Missing the window can cause delays or problems with your case.

MAXIMAX EDE W: Nou ede ranpli I-821, I-765 ak lòt fòm ki ale ak TPS ou.

Tax · IRS Refund Processing Time

Kreyòl: Ranbousman taks ka pran plizyè semèn, sitou si w reklame EITC oswa kredi pou timoun. Sa pa vle di gen pwoblèm otomatikman.

English: Tax refunds may take several weeks, especially if you claimed EITC or child tax credits. This doesn’t always mean there is an issue.

MAXIMAX EDE W: Nou ka ede w konprann mesaj IRS yo epi tcheke estati w.

Benefits · SNAP Rules & Renewal

Kreyòl: Gen moun ki bezwen renouvle SNAP yo alantou menm dat chak ane. Gen nouvo règ pou kèk adilt san timoun, men gen eksepsyon.

English: Many SNAP cases must be renewed around the same time each year. New rules affect some adults without dependents, but there are exceptions.

MAXIMAX EDE W: Nou ede w ranpli aplikasyon, verifikasyon revni ak dokiman sipò.

Housing · Section 8 & Waitlists

Kreyòl: Lis atant Section 8 ka ouvri oswa fèmen selon vil la. Lè lis la ouvri, ou dwe pare ak dokiman w yo pou aplike rapid.

English: Section 8 waiting lists open and close depending on your local housing authority. When the list opens, you must be ready to apply quickly.

MAXIMAX EDE W: Nou ede w prepare aplikasyon lojman ak ranpli fòm sou òdinatè.

Health · Medicaid & Insurance Renewal

Kreyòl: Plizyè moun resevwa lèt pou renouvle Medicaid oswa lòt asirans leta. Si ou pa reponn, ou ka pèdi kouvèti w.

English: Many people receive letters asking them to renew Medicaid or other coverage. Ignoring the notice can cause you to lose your benefits.

MAXIMAX EDE W: Nou ede w konprann lèt yo, telechaje oswa ranpli fòm sou entènèt.

New USCIS Fee Rule Now in Effect

Effective: March 25, 2025

Kreyòl: USCIS mete nouvo frè sou plizyè fòm tankou I-130, I-485, I-765, ak I-821. Gen kèk frè ki monte, kèk ki bese. MAXIMAX ka ede w chèche frè ki kòrèk pou ka pa w.

English: USCIS updated fees for forms like I-130, I-485, I-765 and I-821. Some went up, some went down. MAXIMAX can help calculate the correct fee for your case.

Who is affected: Family petitions · Work permits · Green cards · TPS

Tax · IRS

IRS Refund Processing Time Update

Effective: January 10, 2025

Kreyòl: IRS di ranbousman ka pran 3–5 semèn pou moun ki depoze bonè, sitou si w reklame EITC oswa CTC. Sa pa vle di gen pwoblèm nan dosye a; se tan pwosesis nòmal.

English: IRS says refunds may take 3–5 weeks for early filers, especially if you claimed EITC or CTC. This does not automatically mean there is a problem with your return.

Who is affected: Early filers · Parents · Low-income workers

➤ MAXIMAX can help you check your IRS refund status and explain the messages.
